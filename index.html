About EduEdge

G.D. Goenka Public School is a fantastic place for students to learn and grow. It’s known for its strong focus on both academics and extracurricular activities, which helps students explore their passions. The school has a great community vibe, with dedicated teachers who genuinely care about helping each student succeed. The facilities are top-notch, providing everything needed for a fun and engaging learning experience. Beyond just studying, G.D. Goenka emphasizes the importance of sports, arts, and values, ensuring that students develop into well-rounded individuals. This website was created by Anshuman Giri from 7A.
